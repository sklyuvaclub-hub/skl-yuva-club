About Our Club

Shaheed Krishan Lal Yuva Club Barog

Shaheed Krishan Lal Yuva Club Barog is a social and youth organization dedicated to community welfare, unity and development of society.

The club actively participates in social activities, sports events, cultural programs and village welfare initiatives to inspire the youth and serve society.

Our Mission

To encourage youth participation in social service, cultural preservation and community development while promoting unity and brotherhood.

Our Vision

To build a stronger, united and progressive society through service, dedication and leadership.

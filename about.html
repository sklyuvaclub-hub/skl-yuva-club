About Our Club

Serving Society | Promoting Sports | Inspiring Youth

Who We Are

Shaheed Krishan Lal Yuva Club Barog is a youth organization dedicated to serving society through sports, social service, cultural programs, environmental awareness and national celebrations. Our aim is to unite the youth and encourage leadership, discipline and community service.

Our Mission

To empower youth through sports, education, social service and community development.

Our Vision

To build a responsible, healthy and united society through active youth participation.

Our Objectives

  • ✔ Promote Sports Activities
  • ✔ Organize Blood Donation Camps
  • ✔ Celebrate National Festivals
  • ✔ Tree Plantation Drives
  • ✔ Help Needy Families
  • ✔ Organize Cultural Events
  • ✔ Encourage Youth Leadership
  • ✔ Support Education & Awareness Programs
